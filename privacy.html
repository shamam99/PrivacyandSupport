Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Mentora ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Apple Game Center Player ID and Display Name (for authentication)
  • Gameplay data (scores, achievements, preferences)
  • Uploaded PDFs for question generation (processed temporarily)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage user sessions, rooms, and game progress
  • To generate quiz questions from uploaded PDFs
  • To improve app performance and features

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, except to process PDFs and generate questions via a secure AI model hosted by us.

4. Data Storage

User data is securely stored in our backend using industry-standard practices (e.g., MongoDB Atlas with access control).

5. Contact
